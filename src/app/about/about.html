About Jaya Foundation

Your Story

Founded in 2026, Jaya charitable trust began with a small group of dedicated volunteers serving meals to homeless families and supporting children in need. Witnessing the struggles of poverty, disability, and malnutrition in our communities inspired us to create a lasting solution. Today, we reach thousands of families every month, providing hope and essential support to those who need it most.

Core Values

  • Compassion: We approach every individual with empathy and care, ensuring no one is left behind.
  • Transparency: All donations and operations are conducted with full accountability and openness.
  • Inclusivity: We serve people from all backgrounds, focusing on the most vulnerable.
  • Community-Driven: Our initiatives are built and sustained by local communities and volunteers.

Impact / Achievements

Since our inception, Jaya Foundation has made a significant difference:

  • Over 50,000 meals served to families in need
  • 200+ dedicated volunteers engaged in our programs
  • 10 community kitchens established across the region
  • 500+ children provided with educational and medical support
  • Countless lives uplifted through our donation drives

How You Help

We run comprehensive programs to support the poor and vulnerable:

  • Food Distribution: Daily meal drives and ration kit distributions to families in need.
  • Medical Aid: Free health check-ups and support for disabled and unhealthy children.
  • Educational Support: School supplies, tutoring, and scholarships for underprivileged children.
  • Community Outreach: Awareness campaigns and partnerships with local organizations.
